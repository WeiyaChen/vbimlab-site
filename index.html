Hi, welcome. The vBIM (Virtual/Visual BIM) Lab is dedicated for the theories and applications of BIM (Building Information Modeling), both in coding and data management aspects.

For undergraduate or graduate students, feel free to contact me if you are interested in one of the above topics.

欢迎对工业软件研发、智能设计、工程数据治理感兴趣的同学加入。
